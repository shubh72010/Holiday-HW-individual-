Consumer Protection Laws

  • The Consumer Protection Act, 2019 – Establishes rights and mechanisms for consumer grievance redressal.
  • Legal Metrology Act – Ensures correct weights and measures in trade.
  • Food Safety and Standards Act – Regulates food quality and safety.
  • Drugs and Cosmetics Act – Controls the quality of medicines and cosmetics.
  • Essential Commodities Act – Prevents hoarding and black-marketing.
