Rights and Responsibilities of Consumers

Rights of Consumers:

  • Right to Safety – Protection against hazardous goods.
  • Right to Information – Have knowledge regarding the product (price, contents, expiry).
  • Right to Choose – Having many various goods/services to choose from.
  • Right to be Heard – Complaints must be heard.
  • Right to Redressal – Compensation for unfair trade.
  • Right to Consumer Education – Awareness about rights and laws.

Responsibilities of Consumers:

  • Inform yourself and ask questions prior to buying.
  • Always check MRP, expiry date, and quality marks (ISI, AGMARK).
  • Keep bills and receipts for future claims.
  • File complaints responsibly if cheated.
  • Avoid abuse of consumer rights.
